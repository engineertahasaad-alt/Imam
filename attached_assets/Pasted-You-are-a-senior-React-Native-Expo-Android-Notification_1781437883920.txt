ISSUE #1 — ADHAN NOTIFICATION SHOWS BUT NO AUDIO PLAYS
======================================================

Current behavior:

* Notification popup appears.
* Adhan audio does not play.

Possible cause:

Android notification channels are immutable.
If a channel was originally created without sound, Android ignores future sound updates.

Required investigation:

1. Audit notification channel creation.
2. Audit notification scheduling.
3. Audit audio asset loading.
4. Audit Android-specific notification settings.
5. Audit Expo notification implementation.

Required fixes:

1. Verify all audio files exist and are correctly bundled.

2. Verify paths and asset registration.

3. Verify app.json/app.config configuration.

4. Verify Expo asset bundling.

5. If channels currently use IDs such as:

adhan_alafasy
adhan_makkah
adhan_madani

Replace them with versioned IDs such as:

adhan_makkah_v2
adhan_madani_v2

(or appropriate versioned IDs)

to force Android to create fresh channels.

6. Ensure every channel is created with:

* HIGH importance
* custom sound configured at creation time
* vibration enabled when appropriate
* proper Android settings

7. Verify every scheduled notification uses the correct channel.

8. Add diagnostic logging for:

* channel creation
* sound registration
* scheduling
* trigger execution

9. Verify Expo Go limitations.

10. If Development Build or Production Build is required,
    configure accordingly.

Success criteria:

* Notification appears.
* Correct Adhan audio plays.
* No duplicate notifications.
* No regression.
